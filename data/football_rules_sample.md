# Football Rules Sample

## Handball

Handball decisions may consider whether the player made their body unnaturally bigger with the arm or hand.

## Offside

A player may be in an offside position if they are nearer to the opponent's goal line than both the ball and the second-last opponent when the ball is played.

## Penalty

A penalty kick is awarded when a player commits a direct free kick offense inside their own penalty area.

## Red Card

A player may be sent off for serious foul play, violent conduct, or denying an obvious goal-scoring opportunity.
