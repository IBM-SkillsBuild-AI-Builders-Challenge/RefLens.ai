# Responsible AI

RefLens AI is designed as an educational assistant, not an official referee system.

## Limitations

- It does not access live match video.
- It does not replace official VAR decisions.
- It depends on user-provided match descriptions.
- Confidence levels are approximate.
- Some decisions require camera angles, timing, and referee interpretation.

## Safety Approach

The app avoids absolute claims when evidence is limited.
It explains uncertainty.
It shows why fans may disagree.
It encourages users to treat outputs as educational explanations.
