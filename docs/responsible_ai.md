# Responsible AI

RefLens AI is designed as an educational assistant, not an official referee or VAR system.

## Purpose

The goal of RefLens AI is to help football fans better understand controversial referee and VAR decisions. It explains the rule, the reasoning, the uncertainty, and why fans may disagree.

## Limitations

RefLens AI has important limitations:

- It does not access live match video.
- It does not replace official referees, VAR officials, or competition authorities.
- It depends on the match description provided by the user.
- Some decisions require camera angles, timing, referee interpretation, and official VAR lines.
- Confidence levels are approximate and should not be treated as official judgments.

## Responsible AI Approach

RefLens AI is designed to:

- Avoid claiming certainty when evidence is limited.
- Show a confidence level for every explanation.
- Explain uncertainty clearly.
- Explain why fans may reasonably disagree.
- Use plain language so new fans can understand the decision.
- Provide educational explanations rather than final rulings.

## Human Oversight

Official match decisions must remain with trained referees, VAR officials, and governing bodies. RefLens AI supports fan education but does not override human decision-making.

## Example Responsible Output

```text
Medium confidence — The description suggests the arm may have made the body unnaturally bigger, but a real decision would require video evidence, timing, and referee interpretation.
````
