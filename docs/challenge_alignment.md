# IBM SkillsBuild Challenge Alignment

## Project Name

RefLens AI

## Challenge Goal

RefLens AI supports the IBM SkillsBuild AI Builders Challenge by using AI to help football fans understand the game more clearly.

## Problem

Football fans often see controversial referee and VAR decisions without understanding the rule, reasoning, or uncertainty behind the call. This can lead to confusion, frustration, and mistrust.

## Solution

RefLens AI explains controversial football decisions in clear language. It helps fans understand:

- What rule applies
- Why the decision may be supported
- What uncertainty exists
- Why fans may disagree
- How confident the explanation is

## Supported Decisions

The MVP supports:

- Handball
- Offside
- Penalty
- Red card
- Foul before goal

## IBM Technology Alignment

### IBM Granite

IBM Granite is the planned generative AI model for creating natural-language referee and VAR explanations.

### Langflow

Langflow is used to design the AI workflow from user input to rule retrieval to final explanation.

### Docling

Docling is planned for processing football rule documents into structured text for rule retrieval.

### IBM SkillsBuild

IBM SkillsBuild supports the learning and development process behind the project.

## Responsible AI Fit

RefLens AI does not replace referees. It is designed as an educational assistant that explains uncertainty, avoids absolute claims, and keeps human officials at the center of final decision-making.

## Impact

RefLens AI can make football more understandable, transparent, and accessible for casual fans, new fans, and viewers who want clearer explanations during major tournaments.
