# IBM SkillsBuild Challenge Alignment

## Project Name

RefLens AI

## Challenge Focus

RefLens AI supports the challenge goal of using AI to help football fans understand the game before, during, or after a match.

## Problem

Fans often see referee and VAR decisions without understanding the rule, the reasoning, or the uncertainty behind the call.

## Solution

RefLens AI explains controversial decisions such as handball, offside, penalties, red cards, and fouls before goals in clear language.

## IBM Technology Plan

- IBM Granite: generate natural-language referee explanations
- Langflow: organize the decision explanation workflow
- Docling: process football rule documents into searchable context
- IBM SkillsBuild: support development and learning

## Responsible AI

RefLens AI does not replace official referees. It gives educational explanations, shows uncertainty, and avoids absolute claims when the evidence is limited.
