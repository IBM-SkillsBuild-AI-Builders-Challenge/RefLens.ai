# Demo Script

Hi, this is RefLens AI, an explainable AI companion for football referee and VAR decisions.

Football fans often see controversial calls without understanding the rule or reasoning behind them. RefLens AI solves this by explaining the decision, the rule, the uncertainty, and why fans may disagree.

In this demo, I select Handball. I describe a defender blocking a shot inside the box with an arm extended away from the body.

RefLens AI explains that the penalty is likely supported because the arm may have made the body unnaturally bigger. It also shows a medium confidence score because real VAR decisions require video evidence, timing, and referee interpretation.

The app also explains why fans may disagree, such as short reaction time or camera angle.

RefLens AI uses IBM Granite, Langflow, and Docling to make football decisions more transparent and understandable for fans.
